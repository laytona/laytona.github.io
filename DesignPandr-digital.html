Save the Date designs

With winter upon us next year's summer is a long way off. Unless of course your planning a wedding next year. Designing and sending out a sace the date card is an important part to make sure the people you want at your wedding are given enough notice.

I didn't want to design something too sentitmental, I wanted to design something graphical and unique. The idea to illustrate the couple and the venue was a nice way to visualise what the save the date card is telling you.

The difference in venues provided some unique illustrator challenges as you can see from the decorative St Peter's Church in Ealing. All in all I was happy with the result. The client was able to email the vertical versions to friends and print out the postcard sized ones.
